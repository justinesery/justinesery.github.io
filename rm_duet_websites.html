Restaurant Manager & Duet Websites

The existing Restaurant Manager website had several issues to fix. It was a low converting site with an extraneous amount of pages that the user could easily get lost in, which caused higher abandonment rates. It's non-responsive design and outdated graphics did not speak to the target audience or one of the product's main incentives - that it is easy to use.

Duet was a new product from the same software developers who wanted the two products to be visually related to one another but also be able to stand on their own. Considering this, two separate websites were created with similar themes and color palettes to tie the two sites together. Links were also added on each site to allow the user to easily switch between the two if needed.

Restaurant Manager Website

I developed visitor profiles and worked to uncover core messaging. I surveyed all the pages and pulled out overlaying themes that were present but not concise. I then combined and/or removed expendable pages, providing enough engaging content that the visitor could be informed, but not so much that the visitor would become overwhelmed and lose their path to converting. I also updated the graphics and imagery to show engaging interaction between customers and the product.

Duet POS Website

I started to create the Duet website by preparing visitor profiles and buyers journeys. Based on this, the site was created for the user to learn about the product, download helpful materials, view customer testimonials and finally, guide them to want to speak with a local reseller.

Strong visuals, including customers interacting with the product and screenshots displaying how Duet can resolve common industry pain points, aided in this journey.
